## 2. Data collected

The platform may collect the following data:

- First and last name
- Personal identifier (DNI, RUT, or equivalent)
- Email address
- Gender, when the user decides to provide it
- Data derived from platform usage, such as responses, interactions, and activity

## 3. Purpose of processing

Personal data will be processed for the following purposes:

- User account management
- Access to and use of the platform
- Delivery of educational activities

### Use for research purposes

Data use for research purposes will only occur when the user has provided explicit consent through an informed consent process.

Such consent will be collected in accordance with institutional and ethical protocols of the University of Valladolid.

Whenever possible, data used for research will be anonymized or pseudonymized.

**Important:** Consent for research is not collected through this platform. It is managed using specific instruments approved by ethics committees.

## 4. Legal basis for processing

- Performance of the service requested by the user
- User consent for specific purposes, such as research
- Compliance with applicable legal obligations

## 5. Data retention

User accounts and all associated data will be deleted periodically on the following dates:

- January 1
- July 1

In addition, the system keeps daily backups with a maximum retention period of one week.

## 6. Data location and storage

All data is stored in infrastructure managed by the University of Valladolid.

## 7. Data recipients

Personal data will not be shared with third parties, except when required by law or when necessary in authorized research projects and with prior user consent.

## 8. User rights

Users have the right to:

- Access their personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of their data
- Request restriction of processing
- Object to processing
- Request data portability
- Withdraw consent at any time

## 9. Data security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures have been implemented to protect personal data and prevent loss, alteration, or unauthorized access.

## 10. Changes to this privacy policy

This policy may be updated due to legal or technical changes. Users are encouraged to review it periodically.
